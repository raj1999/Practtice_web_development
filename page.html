History

" Oriental Institute of Science & Technology (OIST) is an engineering college located in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh. It is part of the Oriental Group of Institutes. The college is affiliated to the Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya. Oriental group is ranked amongst the best institutes of Central India with specific focus on skill development and training. OIST Bhopal has consistently produced the best placement statistics in the region and was ranked the best engineering college in Madhya Pradesh. The institute is also famous for high number of start-ups its students have produced over the years. The Institutes of the Group are located in Bhopal, Indore and Jabalpur only. The Institutes of Bhopal and Jabalpur Campus are affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi Proudyogiki Vishwavidyalaya, (RGPV) Bhopal while the Institutes of Indore Campus are affiliated to Oriental University (OU), Indore.[1] Oriental Institute of Science and Technology is the most preferred choice of the JEE/PET/AIEEE/MAT/Pre-MCA selected students, seeking admission through counseling conducted by the State Universities and MHRD.[2] OIST, Bhopal has all the eligible programs CSE, IT, EC, EX, AU, ME,CIVIL and MCA accredited by the National Board of Accreditation AICTE, New Delhi."

Admissions

OIST offers B.Tech, M.Tech and MCA programs and is considered the top choice among IITJEE/MAT/Pre MCA candidates in the state. The institute offers seven different specialization options in engineering.
  • B. Tech 1st year admission is based on state level counseling through JEE Main as well as college-level counseling on the basis of percentage secured in class 12th exam.
  • It is mandatory for applicants to register online with the Directorate of Technical Education here for the counseling process in order to be considered for admission at OIST.
  • Candidates may apply through online as well as offline modes. For admission to B.E program, a valid score in PET conducted by M.P state or JEE Main is required.
  • The institute also offers admission in M.Tech and MCA programs on the basis of scores of GATE and Pre-MCA examination.
  • The Oriental Institute of Science and Technology has been in operation since 1995 and located in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh. OIST is affiliated to the Rajiv Gandhi Praudyogikee Vishwavidyalaya (RGPV) – the state technological university of Madhya Pradesh. Scholarship options are available for reserved category domiciled candidates of MP.
As per the students reviews on collegedunia.com, the highest package offered to students of OIST was 4LPA in the year 2019-2020. The top recruiters visited OIST Bhopal include TCS, BYJU,S, Mahindra, Tata Motors, Amazon, Wipro, HCL, Capgemini, Cognizant, Zensar, Nucleus software Etc.
